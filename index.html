Process

From setup to
harvest in 4 steps

1

Set up your farm profile

Enter your farm area (up to 50m²), location in San Pablo City, and your preferred farming schedule.

2

Visualize your layout with AR

Use the AR feature to place virtual beds and trellises in your actual space before you commit to digging.

3

Accept AI crop recommendations

Review and accept the AI-generated crop cycle. SoilSync handles your entire planting timeline from there.

4

Follow SMS reminders & harvest

Receive daily task reminders via SMS and track your progress until harvest day with the calendar view.

Research objectives

What we set out to achieve

SoilSync is built on six specific research objectives, each evaluated through rigorous testing and validation.

01

AR Farm Area Planning

Validated through farmer surveys measuring perceived usefulness and accuracy of AR visualization.

02

AI Crop Planning

Validated against DA organic crop planning standards for accuracy and relevance of recommendations.

03

SMS Automation

Evaluated through functional testing to verify accurate and timely delivery of farming task notifications.

04

Farming Modules

Validated by the DA to ensure completeness and alignment with official organic farming guidelines.

05

User-Friendly Interface

Assessed through SUS surveys administered to farmer-users to measure ease of use and satisfaction.

06

System Effectiveness

Evaluated via quasi-experimental study measuring knowledge gain, task compliance, and learner satisfaction.

Who it's for

Three groups that benefit
from SoilSync

👨‍🌾

Small-Scale Farmers

The primary beneficiaries. SoilSync provides practical guidance and structured knowledge to help farmers adopt, understand, and efficiently manage organic farming practices.

Department of Agriculture

SoilSync serves as a digital platform for the DA to promote programs, distribute organic farming protocols, and monitor adoption across San Pablo City.

The broader San Pablo City community gains access to healthier, chemical-free organic produce, improving food security and public health outcomes for all.
